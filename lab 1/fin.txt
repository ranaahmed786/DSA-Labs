A computer network is a system that connects multiple devices to share resources and information
It allows communication between computers, servers, and other devices using wired or wireless connections
Networks can be classified into different types, such as LAN, WAN, and MAN, based on their size and purpose
The Internet is the largest network, linking millions of computers worldwide. Data is transmitted using protocols like TCP/IP, ensuring smooth communication
Security measures, such as firewalls and encryption, protect data from cyber threats
Networking plays a vital role in businesses, education, and daily life
It improves efficiency, collaboration, and access to information
